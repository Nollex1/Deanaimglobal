HOW IT WORKS

The team structure explained

DEAN AIM Global runs on a binary network system. You sit at the centre. Every person you bring in goes either left or right. When both sides match — you earn. And it keeps growing.

BINARY NETWORK — DEAN AIM GLOBAL

1

You Join

Pick a package. Your account(s) go live within 1 working day. Your mentor is assigned immediately.

2

You Build Left & Right

Every person you bring in goes to your left or right leg. You earn a Direct Bonus instantly for each one.

3

Left = Right = You Earn

When activity on your left matches your right, the system pays you a Matched Sales Bonus automatically.

4

Your Team Builds Their Teams

Your recruits recruit others. You earn Unilevel income from up to 10 levels deep — paid monthly.

5

8 Streams Flow Simultaneously

Direct Bonus, Matched Sales, Promo, Unilevel, Stairstep, Royalty, Rebates, and Gift Innovation all pay you as your network grows.
